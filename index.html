About Eigen AI Company

Company Profile

Eigen AI is a professional financial information consulting enterprise registered in England, located at 92 Moulsham St, Chelmsford. We integrate artificial intelligence, quantitative finance and macroeconomic research to provide one-stop financial data consulting services for global capital market participants.

Since establishment in 2018, we have focused on solving information asymmetry problems in the financial industry, using machine learning and natural language processing to sort out massive news, stock trends, macro indicators and corporate financial statements, converting raw data into actionable decision-making references. Our client base covers over 200 enterprises across Europe, including asset management firms, fintech startups, and multinational corporations.

We hold a full set of financial consulting qualifications recognized by the UK Financial Conduct Authority (FCA), and our data security system complies with GDPR regulations, ensuring the confidentiality and compliance of client data throughout the service process.

Corporate Mission & Vision

Our Financial Consulting Services

Service Principles

All our services adhere to three core principles: Data Accuracy (multi-source verification and real-time calibration), Regulatory Compliance (fully aligned with FCA/MiFID II requirements), and Customization (tailored to client's business scale and risk appetite).

Global Market Real-Time Data Consulting

Real-time quotes, capital flow monitoring, sector rotation tracking covering stocks, foreign exchange, commodities and bond markets worldwide. Our data sources include LSE, NYSE, NASDAQ, and over 50 global exchanges, updated every 100ms for high-frequency trading clients.

Key Features: Customizable data dashboards, historical trend comparison, cross-market correlation analysis, abnormal price alert system.

Enterprise Financial Risk Assessment

AI quantitative risk scoring, credit evaluation, supply chain financial risk early warning for small, medium and large UK enterprises. Our model incorporates 120+ risk factors including cash flow, debt ratio, industry volatility, and macroeconomic indicators.

Deliverables: Comprehensive risk report, risk mitigation recommendations, quarterly risk review meetings.

Investment Portfolio AI Optimization

Asset allocation simulation, backtesting analysis, volatility control and long-term return forecasting for institutional and private wealth clients. Our optimization model uses Monte Carlo simulation to stress-test portfolios against 100+ market scenarios.

Client Types: High-net-worth individuals (minimum asset threshold £500k), family offices, pension funds, hedge funds.

Financial News Sentiment Analysis

Automatically extract positive/negative sentiment from financial news, social media and official policy documents to predict short-term market sentiment swings. Our NLP model processes 500,000+ financial news articles daily in 15 languages.

Use Cases: Short-term trading signal generation, brand reputation monitoring for listed companies, policy impact assessment.

Cross-Border Regulatory Consulting

Professional interpretation of FCA, EU MiFID II and other financial supervision rules for cross-border investment institutions operating in UK. Our regulatory team includes former FCA auditors and EU financial policy advisors.

Services Include: Compliance gap analysis, regulatory filing assistance, staff training on regulatory requirements.

Custom AI Financial System Deployment

Private deployment of exclusive financial data analysis AI platform, customized data interface and report generation module for enterprise clients. Deployment options include cloud-based (AWS/Azure) and on-premise solutions.

Implementation Cycle: 4-8 weeks for standard customization, 12-16 weeks for deep customization; includes 12 months of free maintenance and updates.
